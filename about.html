BIO

My name is Christopher Ford and I am a full-stack Web Application Developer currently living in Waco, Texas. My primary focus and inspiration for my studies is Web Development. In my free time, I study web designing techniques and best practices. I am both driven and self-motivated, and I am constantly experimenting with new technologies and techniques. I am very passionate about Web Development, and strive to better myself as a developer, and the development community as a whole.


I have been studying web development since 2011 and I develop in React. I am experienced in both front and back-end development, and I am proficient in HTML/HTML5, CSS/CSS3, SASS, JavaScript, jQuery, and AJAX, to name a few. I am also proficient in many non-web-based languages, including but not limited to MongoDB, Webpack, Docker, C/C++, and MATLAB. I have also worked on some Python projects, and have used the language to create one-time use tools for data processing and similar purposes
